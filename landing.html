AI Agents

Meet the Agents

Five specialized AI agents powered by the SixthSense™ Engine, each designed to solve a unique challenge for game developers.

Rx
Deep Session Inspector
Radiologist

Surfaces relevant gameplay clips in response to a developer's investigation. Inspects individual sessions with precision — like a medical radiologist examining imaging data.

Or
Behavioral Intelligence
Oracle

Learns by watching gameplay at scale. Develops meta-level understanding of player behavior, friction points, emergent strategies, and behavioral trends.

Fc
Predictive Market Research
Forecaster

Runs population-scale experiments using AI players with distinct personas. Predicts how real players respond to balance changes before release.

Co
Personalized Player Guide
Coach

Observes individual gameplay and provides personalized guidance. Helps players improve performance without changes to game logic or design.

Gd
UA Fraud Detection & Recovery
Guardian

Analyzes gameplay by channels to expose fraud and converts suspicious traffic into verifiable video proof to recover wasted UA spend.
